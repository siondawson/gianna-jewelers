About Us

John Giannakopoulos founded Gianna Jewelers in 2009 with a vision to offer the Cleveland/Akron area high quality, creative jewelry in a warm and inviting atmosphere.

The foundation of GIANNA JEWELERS is Service. GIANNA JEWELERS continues to set the standard for quality and service to all our valued customers. Our staff can accommodate every request whether it is a simple jewelry repair or a custom designed piece made especially for you. Our certified Watchmaker can provide our customers with superior service and reliability. Big or small, simple or complex, we provide the same level of care, expertise and courtesy to all.
