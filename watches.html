Watches

Gianna Jewelers proudly presents an extensive array of meticulously crafted timepieces, meticulously chosen to cater to various preferences and needs. From the refined precision of Tissot to the contemporary allure of Skagen, our collection offers a diverse selection of watches designed to meet your expectations. Whether you're in search of a traditional dress watch, a functional chronograph, or a versatile everyday timepiece, our curated range has something to suit every taste and requirement. Explore our selection today and find the ideal watch to complement your individual style..

Tissot

Tissot has been pioneering craftsmanship and innovation since its foundation in 1853. For over 155 years, the company has had its home in the Swiss watch making town of Le Locle in the Jura Mountains but now has a presence in over 150 countries. As official timekeeper and partner of NASCAR®, FIBA, AFL, CBA, MotoGP and the World Championships of cycling, fencing and ice hockey, Tissot is committed to respecting tradition, underlining its core values of performance, precision and setting new standards. At Gianna Jewelers in Cleveland, OH, we offer a stunning collection of men and women’s Tissot watches for all occasions.

Skagen

The Skagen Denmark Collections reflect owners Charlotte and Henrick Jorst’s creative Danish spirit with clean, elegant designs, skillful craftsmanship and technical perfection. Skagen watches are constructed of stainless steel and titanium and are available for men and women at Gianna Jeweler’s in Cleveland, OH. Skagen offers many options which include 3ATM water resistance, ultra-slim, diamonds, Swiss movement, dual-time function, chronograph and automatic. Each watch is an unbeatable combination of great design, high quality and affordable prices.

Estate Watches

Estate watches embody timeless elegance, reflecting the refined tastes of their previous owners. Each piece tells a unique story, showcasing exquisite craftsmanship and enduring style. At Gianna Jewelers in Cleveland, OH, we offer a curated selection of estate watches for both men and women. Crafted from premium materials such as stainless steel and titanium, these watches exude sophistication and durability. Our collection includes a variety of features, including water resistance, slim designs, diamond accents, Swiss movement, dual-time function, chronographs, and automatic movements. With their unparalleled combination of superior design, exceptional quality, and accessible prices, estate watches from Gianna Jewelers are a testament to timeless luxury.
